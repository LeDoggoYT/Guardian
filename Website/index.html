FAQ

Questions before you invite Guardian?

Short answers for teams comparing Guardian with command-first moderation bots.

Is Guardian only for large Discord servers?

No. Guardian is built for any server that wants predictable moderation, from private communities to large support spaces.

Can we keep our existing roles and channels?

Yes. Guardian layers over your current structure and lets you map permissions, alerts, ticket routing, and logs to the places your team already uses.

Does Guardian replace human moderators?

No. It removes repetitive moderation work and gives humans better context for the decisions that need judgment.

Can automod rules be tuned?

Yes. Rules can be adjusted by channel, role, severity, cooldown, and escalation path.
